FitDash Manual

Fitdash is an app designed to simplify and even somewhat gamify self-care and fitness.

With this app, you can view your consistency in logons, generate a strength-training routine, and even determine your maintainance calories!

Simply enter your age, height, and weight, and the app will calculate your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) and the amount of calories you need to maintain your current weight.

As well, each and every value is saved upon editing it, so there's no need to re-enter info on a reload of the page - simply see what you've logged and use it freely!

To track calories, we recommend a 3rd party app such as Cronometer or MyFitnessPal. Have fun, and good luck!
